Frequently Asked Questions

How often is a long-term construction rental serviced?

Our standard agreement for a porta potty business rental includes once-weekly service. This includes pumping out the tank, cleaning all surfaces, restocking toilet paper and hand sanitizer, and recharging the unit with deodorizer. More frequent service is available for high-traffic sites.

Can you lift a porta potty over a fence?

Unfortunately, no. Our trucks are equipped with a dolly system for rolling the units into place. We cannot lift them over fences, walls, or up flights of stairs. We require a clear access path of at least 4 feet wide.

Are your porta potties ADA-compliant?

Yes, we offer wheelchair-accessible units that meet ADA guidelines and are built with non-slip floors and handrails.

Can I rent portable toilets for just one day?

Absolutely. We offer daily rentals for private parties, small events, and short-term needs.

Can you place a unit on my sloped driveway?

We cannot place a unit on a significant slope for safety reasons. However, our experienced drivers are experts at finding a viable, level spot, often using shims or blocks to create a stable base on slight inclines.
